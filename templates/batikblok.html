Batik Blok

Create Now!

Batik blok is a traditional type of Malaysian batik produced using wooden or metal blocks to print patterns on fabric. This technique is one of the earliest batik techniques in Malaysia and is used by the Malay community to create sarongs. Nature-inspired motifs are often used in this batik.
